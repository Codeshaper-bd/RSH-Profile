RSH RIM Associates
Quality Accessories, Exporting Excellence

The company is a highly successful garments accessories manufacturing enterprise in Bangladesh, specializing in 100% export-oriented operations. The factory, situated in Surabari, Kashimpur, Gazipur, is well-established and renowned in the industry. It produces 350,000 pieces of accessories daily, utilizing raw materials such as yarn, rubber flex, craft paper, and chemical resins. The production is carried out using 78 machines of various brands. Additionally, the company is proud to be certified under the Global Recycled Standard (GRS) (Version 4.0), reflecting its commitment to sustainable practices.
